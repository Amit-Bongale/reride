Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying with ReRide.

Why buy from ReRide?
Because we aren't just a platform; we are bike enthusiasts. Every bike undergoes a 120-point inspection, comes with a warranty, and ensures you aren't buying someone else's problem.
Can I book a bike online?
Absolutely. Browse our inventory, select your dream machine, and pay a fully refundable token amount to reserve it. Visit our hub for a test ride before sealing the deal.
Do you offer financing?
Yes, we partner with leading banks to offer instant EMI approvals covering up to 85% of the bike's value. Minimal documentation required.
What about RTO transfer?
We handle all the paperwork. From ownership transfer to insurance updates, our team ensures the bike is legally yours without you running around government offices.
