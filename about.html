Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying with ReRide.

"Why should I buy a used bike from Re-Ride?
With expert verified/evaluated bikes as good as new, Prakruthi Re-Ride assures users of secure, quick and easy buying options that have been backed up with a warranty and RC transfer. With multiple benefits such as finance options, expert support and extraordinary customer service, Prakruthi Re-Ride gives you the smartest way to own your next ride.
Can I book a bike online?
Buying a used bike through Prakruthi Re-Ride starts online, but the final step happens in-store. This option allows the user to browse through refurbished bikes and select their choice according to their liking. By visiting the closest Prakruthi Re-Ride showroom, our team will be able to help you with all the further advancements like comparisons, finance options, and paperwork, all under one roof.
Can I sell my bike online through your platform?
Yes. Selling your bike with the help of Prakruthi Re-Ride will be very easy. For this process to take place without any hassles, the user must upload your bike details and photos. The team will get an expert verified inspection and then give you a fair market price and take care of all the paperwork from there on.
Can I exchange my bike or scooter in Re-Ride?
Absolutely. Re-Ride makes exchange of bike very simple. Bring in the old bike for inspection and evaluation and upgrade to any bike model of your preference. We offer fair exchange value, quick processing, and handle all the formalities, so your next ride is just a ride away.
Are the used bikes listed on your platform inspected and certified?
Yes. Every pre-owned bike at Re-Ride is thoroughly inspected across quality checkpoints by trained professionals. We refurbish each and every single bike so that it meets the standards of a new bike, in terms of safety, performance and appearance.
What documents are required to purchase a used bike?
To buy a second-hand bike from Prakruthi Re-Ride, you’ll need a valid government ID (like Aadhaar, PAN, or DL), recent address proof, and a passport-size photo. For financing options, income proof may be required. We take care of the RC transfer and paperwork to make things hassle-free for you.
Is it possible to take a test ride before purchasing a second-hand bike?
Yes. You can visit your nearest Prakruthi Re-Ride showroom to test ride any refurbished two-wheeler before making a decision. Our team will make sure to engage with you and inform you regarding your options, comparisons and help you choose your dream ride.
Do you offer financing options for second-hand bikes?
Yes, Prakruthi Re-Ride offers easy two-wheeler finance plans for used bikes. With minimal effort from the user’s end, our team at Prakruthi Re-Ride will make sure to walk you through the multiple EMI options online and/or at our showroom so you can buy your dream bike without much delay.
What should I do if I face any issues after purchasing a used bike?
Your security is our priority. If you face any issues after purchasing the refurbished bike, you can contact our top-notch customer service right away or you can also visit our nearest Prakruthi Re-Ride outlet. Our team will take care of the rest.
How is the value of my bike determined before selling?
The value of the bike is determined by the following key factors: Your bike's resale value is calculated using several factors such as brand, model, age, mileage, condition, and service history. Prakruthi Re-Ride uses an approach that combines market trends, demand forecasts, and city-specific pricing to offer a fair and transparent used bike valuation. We strive to bring you the best price.
Do you offer doorstep inspection or pickup services for selling bikes?
Yes, Prakruthi Re-Ride offers doorstep inspection if requested. The inspection will be carried out by professionals who will evaluate the bike and give a fair price for it on spot based on the current bike trends and market rates. It’s all designed to make selling simple, smooth, and convenient for you.
